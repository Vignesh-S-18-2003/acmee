ACMEE 2023 BRIEF REPORT

ACMEE 2023, India's Premier International Machine Tools Show, 15th in the series, was successfully organised in Chennai Trade Centre, Chennai during 15-19 June 2023, by Ambattur Industrial Estate Manufacturers' Association, Chennai.

Highlights of the Show are:
1. Live Demonstrations.
2. Product Launches.
3. Finance Lounge - One spot for Exhibitors, Visitors (buyers), Financial Institutions to finalize business.
4. Global Manufacturing Conclave in which 3 leading industry professionals made presentations.

ACMEE 2023, India’s Premier International Machine Tools Show, 15th in the series, was successfully organised in Chennai Trade Centre, Chennai during 15-19 June 2023, by Ambattur Industrial Estate Manufacturers’ Association, Chennai.

A record number of 435 participants including 105 International brands participated in the event spread over 19500 sqm space in seven exhibition halls.

Hon’ble Governor of Tamil Nadu, Thiru R.N. Ravi, inaugurated ACMEE 2023 at a glittering function on 15th June 2023.

Thiru Satyakam Arya, Managing Director & CEO, Daimler India Commercial Vehicles Pvt Ltd and Thiru Satish Sadasivan, Managing Director, Schunk Intek India were the Guests of Honour at the Inaugural Function.

In tune with the event’s theme of Smart Manufacturing, the participants presented a wide variety of machine tools adapting information technology in machinery designed to monitor production processes, automate manufacturing and the use of data analytics to improve manufacturing processes, cost reduction, time saving and mass production.


Global Manufacturing Conclave in which 3 leading industry professionals made presentations.


International Buyers-ACMEE 2023 Exhibitors Meet organised jointly by ACMEE 2023 and EEPC India on 16th and 17th June 2023. 51 Overseas buyers were invited by EEPC for this program.


Ministry of MSME, Government of India, approved ACMEE 2023 for the purpose of extending financial support to participating MSME units.


Publicity campaign on ACMEE 2023 was specially designed to attract appropriate visitors viz decision makers, purchase professionals, R&D professionals, engineers and management Teams. Communication channels were social media, personalised invitations to top industry professionals after collecting data from a number of sources, besides the usual ads in print and electronic media. This paid rich dividends and as per the registration details a record number of 32,550 business visitors visited the event.


Another important feature is the visit of 18 CEOs and decision makers of leading Manufacturing companies as Special Guests of Honour during the course of the 5-day event. They were accompanied by their Teams of Professionals.


As per the feedback received from Participants at the conclusion of the event, 99% of the participants were very happy with the business generated at ACMEE 2023 and have indicated their willingness to participate in the next edition of ACMEE scheduled during 21-24 June 2025. The participants were very happy to receive quality visitors at the Show. The visitors were equally thrilled to see the Machine Tools and Technology relevant to their scheme of manufacturing. A win-win situation for both the exhibitors and visitors.


It was really a very satisfying experience to receive the excellent positive feedback from both the exhibitors and visitors alike… all pointing to the hard work and devotion of the ACMEE 2023 Team.


Success of any exhibition is directly dependent on the quality of exhibitors and their presentations, quality of visitors and their intentions and the ability of the organizers in organising the event. ACMEE 2023 is very fortunate in achieving the best in all these aspects.
